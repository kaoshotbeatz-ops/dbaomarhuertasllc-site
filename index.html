Our story

The story behind the standard.

I was told no more times than I can count. The one yes that mattered didn’t see a résumé — it saw a person. I’ve carried that ever since.
Omar Huertas Founder & ServiceNow Architect
Former architect at Elite ServiceNow partners · $1B+ platforms

I was born into technology — I built my first computer at seven, while my stepfather worked for Wang, Northern Telecom, and Oxford. Growing up Puerto Rican on Long Island, I learned early what it means to be underestimated. The aptitude was always there; the world just needed convincing.

So I set out to prove it. At 31 I went back to school and earned an Associate of Applied Science in Network Technology, then stacked the certifications to back it — Cisco CCNA, CompTIA A+, Lean Six Sigma, ServiceNow, and Microsoft. Not because the work needed them, but because the world asked for proof.

A few people saw it before the paper did. Brian gave me my first real opportunity. Mike Lombardo — the Mayor of ServiceNow — showed me the standard, and the GlideFast community he built showed me what it means to treat people right. I’ve carried that into every engagement since, delivering on billion-dollar enterprise platforms.

Now I’ve started my own — Omar Huertas LLC. Not for the biggest logo in the room, but for honest work, done right, for the people and teams the industry overlooks. Skill first. Honest work always.
