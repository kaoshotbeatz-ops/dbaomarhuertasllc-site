Our story

The horror story of being underestimated.

I was told no more times than I can count. The one yes that mattered didn’t see a résumé — it saw a person. I’ve carried that ever since.
Omar Huertas Founder & ServiceNow Architect
Former architect at Elite ServiceNow partners · $1B+ platforms

Being underestimated isn’t a line on a slide. It’s walking into rooms that already decided who you were — for where you came from, for not fitting the mold. It’s doing the work twice as well and still being told to prove it. It’s the failures nobody saw, and the nights I got back up because giving up was never a luxury I had.

Then one person looked past all of it. They didn’t ask me to be someone else — they handed me the chance and let my work speak. That one yes changed everything. It’s the reason I do this: to be that yes for the next person the world decides to overlook.

I earned the certifications because the world demanded proof. They never changed how I treat the people who trust me. I gave my best long before any company validated it — and I give my whole heart now. The same heart I poured into billion-dollar platforms, I pour into every family and every future behind the work.

I spent years as an architect at two Elite ServiceNow partners. Now I’ve started my own — just registered, just beginning. Not for the biggest logo in the room. For honest work, done right, for the people who deserve it. Nothing more. Nothing less.
