Our story

The horror story of being underestimated.

I was told no more times than I can count. The one yes that mattered didn’t see a résumé — it saw a person. I’ve carried that ever since.
Omar Huertas Founder & ServiceNow Architect
Former architect at Elite ServiceNow partners · $1B+ platforms

I built my first computer at seven. My stepfather worked for Wang, Northern Telecom, and Oxford — technology was in our house before I understood how rare that was. We grew up on Long Island, Puerto Rican in a place that made sure we knew we didn’t belong. They mocked my name. They told me what I’d never be.

The world wasn’t gentle. I’ve been spit on, jumped, stabbed, wrongly accused — told I’d never be more than a garbage man. There was a lot of pain before any success. But giving up was never a luxury I had, so I kept getting back up.

At 31, I went back to school and earned my CCNA. Brian didn’t see where I came from — he saw the work, and he took me for real. That was the first real yes. Mike Lombardo, the Mayor of ServiceNow, showed me the standard. I never forgot either of them.

I earned every certification because the world demanded proof — never because the work needed it. They never changed how I treat the people who trust me. I give my whole heart now — the same heart I poured into billion-dollar platforms and into the families and futures behind the work.

Now I’ve started my own — just registered, just beginning. Not for the biggest logo in the room. For honest work, done right, for the people the world overlooks. Nothing more. Nothing less.
