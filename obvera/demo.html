Common questions

The honest version.

What's free vs paid?

Service Desk · Knowledge · Assets · Compliance Baselines · App Catalog · Profile Templates are free forever up to 5 users. Devices is $4/device/mo from device #1. Everything else is per-module SKUs — see pricing.

Is my data isolated from other tenants?

Yes. Each tenant gets its own dedicated MariaDB database on AWS RDS. Tables are not shared. A query in tenant A literally cannot read tenant B's bytes — different DB. Secrets live in per-tenant envelope-encrypted vaults that the platform owner cannot decrypt.

Where does my data live?

AWS us-east-1 today (with HA standby in us-east-1b). EU and AU regions are on the roadmap. Backups stay in the same region unless you ask otherwise.

What happens if Obvera dies?

Both EC2 origins run identical code. If one dies, the other carries (Cloudflare LB auto-failover is being wired this quarter). Your data is on RDS with automated daily backups + delete-guard snapshots + per-tenant export jobs. If we ever shut the company down, every tenant gets a full DB export and 90 days to migrate.

Can I bring my own integrations (Slack, Jira, Cloudflare, etc)?

Yes — the Integrations Registry has a typed catalog of 8 providers today (Cloudflare, Slack, Jira, ServiceNow, Azure, Arlo, Home Assistant, Gmail) with self-serve token entry. Credentials go straight to your tenant's vault.

Can I migrate FROM ServiceNow / Jamf / Jira?

Yes — Omar has 20 years of ServiceNow experience and can run the migration as a paid engagement. For Jamf, MDM enrollment doesn't transfer between MDM servers (Apple constraint), so devices re-enroll over a refresh window.

Do you do SOC 2 / HIPAA / PCI / ISO?

Obvera ships with live compliance posture mapping (SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, NIST CSF, PCI DSS, HIPAA) computed from real platform evidence. Third-party audit not yet performed; on the roadmap for Q4 2026. Full trust details ›

Who's behind this?

Omar Huertas. 20 years in IT, Senior ServiceNow Developer, 32 SN certifications, CCNP, VCP, CISSP. At ServiceNow Knowledge every year since 2017. About ›

Where's the source code? Is this open source?

Source is proprietary today (we operate the platform). Standalone deployable versions for on-prem buyers are on the roadmap as a separate product.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Anytime. You get a 30-day window to export your data. No retention games.
