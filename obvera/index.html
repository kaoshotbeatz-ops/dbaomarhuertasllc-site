Built right

Multi-tenant. HA. Audit-grade.

Multi-tenant
Per-tenant database isolation on MariaDB / RDS. Per-tenant vault. Per-tenant audit chain. One tenant cannot read another's bytes.
HA across AZs
Two AWS EC2 origins in different Availability Zones. Auto-rollback on failed deploy. Promote ships to both boxes in one command.
Tamper-evident audit
Every privileged action is hash-chained into a Truth Ledger. The verifier walks the chain and detects content OR structural tampering.
ServiceNow-grade RBAC
Roles × Groups × Personas. 12 seeded personas (CIO, CISO, Help Desk Agent, Procurement Mgr, …). ACL inspector explains every allow/deny.
Domain separation
Sub-orgs inside a tenant for parent+subsidiary or MSP-under-one-install. ServiceNow's domain separation, fully ported.
Service Map (CSDM-class)
15-lane interactive SVG of the whole estate — edge, AWS, on-prem, identity, virtualization, monitoring, every tenant's integrations + modules + CIs + grants.
