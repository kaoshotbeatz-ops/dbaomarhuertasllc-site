Accessibility

Built for everyone.

Accessibility isn’t a checkbox here — it’s personal. This site is designed to work for every visitor, however you browse.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Our commitment

Omar Huertas LLC is committed to digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and we treat accessibility as an ongoing practice, not a one-time task.

What we’ve built in

  • Built-in accessibility toolbar — tap the ♿ button (bottom-left of every page) to turn on High contrast, Larger text, or Reduce motion. Your choices are remembered on your device.
  • Keyboard support — every interactive element is reachable by keyboard, with a visible focus indicator and a “Skip to content” link.
  • Color contrast — text and controls meet WCAG AA contrast ratios; a high-contrast mode is available.
  • Motion — animations respect your system “Reduce Motion” setting and can be paused manually.
  • Media — video includes descriptive captions and labels; images include meaningful alternative text.
  • Structure — semantic headings, landmarks, and labels for assistive technology.

Ongoing work

Accessibility is never “done.” We continue to test with keyboards, screen readers, and real devices, and we welcome feedback that helps us do better.

Tell us

If you encounter any barrier on this site, please email omar@dbaomarhuertasllc.com and we’ll work to fix it promptly and, if needed, provide the information another way.
