Privacy Policy

How Omar Huertas LLC collects, uses, shares, and protects information submitted through this website.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Omar Huertas LLC (“Omar Huertas LLC,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information in connection with this website (dbaomarhuertasllc.com). By using this site or contacting us through it, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to provide. When you submit our contact form or email us, that may include your name, email address, company, phone number, and the contents of your message. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information.

Our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) for security and reliability. We do not use this data to identify individual visitors.

2. How we use information

We use the information you provide to: respond to your inquiry; discuss, scope, and deliver consulting services; send business communications you request; and maintain records of our correspondence. We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

3. How we share information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only: (a) with trusted service providers who help us operate the business (for example, email or hosting providers) under confidentiality obligations; (b) when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; or (c) to protect the rights, safety, or property of Omar Huertas LLC, our clients, or others. Client information handled during engagements is treated as confidential and governed by the applicable engagement agreement.

4. Cookies & analytics

This is a static website and does not set advertising or tracking cookies by default. If we add analytics or similar tools in the future, we will update this policy to describe them and any choices available to you.

5. Data retention

We keep inquiry and correspondence information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, after which it is deleted or anonymized. You may ask us to delete your information at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to opt out of certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@dbaomarhuertasllc.com and we will respond as required by applicable law. You may also unsubscribe from business emails at any time by replying with “unsubscribe.”

8. Third-party links

This site may link to third-party websites (for example, ServiceNow or LinkedIn). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites; we encourage you to review their policies.

9. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for business audiences and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent changes. Material changes will be posted on this page.

11. Contact us

Questions or requests regarding this policy can be sent to contact@dbaomarhuertasllc.com.
